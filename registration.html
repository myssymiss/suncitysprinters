Safety, Hydration & Weather Policy

  • Practices include approximately 45 minutes of structured warm-up, stretching, mobility, and injury prevention training.
  • Athletes must arrive prepared with large water bottles, proper footwear, and weather-appropriate clothing.
  • Athletes are expected to arrive fueled and hydrated.
  • Practices will only be canceled for lightning or sustained winds over 30 mph.
Parent/Guardian Initials

Code of Conduct

  • Show respect toward coaches, teammates, officials, competitors, and facilities.
  • Follow all safety instructions and training guidelines.
  • Maintain positive sportsmanship at practices and competitions.
  • Refrain from bullying, profanity, disrespectful behavior, or unsafe conduct.
  • Attend at least two (2) practices per week. Athletes selected for relay events are expected to attend at least three (3) practices per week.
  • Communicate absences in advance whenever possible.

Athlete Initials

Fundraising Participation & Nonprofit Support Policy

Sun City Sprinters is a nonprofit organization that relies on fundraising to support team operations, equipment, travel assistance, and athlete development opportunities.

  • Athletes and families are expected to participate in scheduled team fundraising activities.
  • Eligibility for financial assistance or travel support may be prioritized for families who actively participate in fundraising.
  • Funds raised are used to benefit the team as a whole and are not guaranteed to offset individual expenses.
Parent/Guardian Initials

Relay & Travel Commitment Policy

  • Relay selection represents a commitment to the team and competition schedule.
  • By accepting a relay position, the athlete and family are committing to attend and compete and may not withdraw once committed, except in the case of injury, illness, or family emergency.
  • Withdrawing from a relay after commitment may impact future relay selection and team opportunities.

Required: USATF Youth Membership Registration

All athletes must complete their USATF Youth Membership registration before participating in practices or competitions.

  1. Visit www.usatf.org and create a parent account.
  2. Create a youth athlete profile for your child.
  3. Purchase the USATF Youth Membership.
  4. Add Sun City Sprinters as the athlete’s club affiliation.
  5. Upload your child’s birth certificate to the athlete profile for age verification.
