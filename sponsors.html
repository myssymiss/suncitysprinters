Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To provide comprehensive track and field programs that develop athletes of all skill levels, promote excellence and character, and build a lifelong love for the sport while serving as a positive force in the El Paso community through outreach and mentorship.

Vision

We envision a future where young athletes in El Paso have access to structured, supportive, and inspiring track and field opportunities that promote confidence, discipline, teamwork, and leadership—on and off the track.

Sponsorship Letter

Dear Community Partner,

Sun City Sprinters is a youth track and field program based in El Paso, Texas. We are proud to be a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to giving young athletes a positive place to train, grow, and build confidence through sport.

Your sponsorship helps reduce barriers for families by supporting meet fees and event fees, training equipment, and travel assistance when our athletes qualify for championship competitions. With the right support, our kids can compete at a high level while representing El Paso with pride.

We would be honored to partner with you this season. Sponsorship options are listed below. Every contribution supports youth athletes and strengthens our community.

Thank you for considering Sun City Sprinters. We appreciate your support and look forward to partnering with you.

Sincerely,
Coach Marrissa Cammisa
