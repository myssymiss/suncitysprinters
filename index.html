Our Vision

Fueling dreams, one stride at a time

We envision a future where young athletes in El Paso have access to structured, supportive, and inspiring track and field opportunities that promote physical fitness, confidence, discipline, and teamwork.

Through community support, mentorship, and intentional development, Sun City Sprinters exists to remove barriers, uplift potential, and help athletes grow into strong competitors and responsible leaders on and off the track.

At Sun City Sprinters, I coach because I truly love track and field and believe in doing things the right way. I started this program to give athletes a safe and positive place to learn, build confidence, and grow strong habits.

I focus on teaching strong basics, setting clear expectations, and training in ways that fit each athlete‚Äôs age and ability. I also ran track myself, so I understand what it feels like to train, compete, and work toward goals, and I bring that experience into every practice.

Sun City Sprinters is a nonprofit team that serves our community and helps give kids access to quality coaching in a positive environment. Our goal is to help athletes grow, build character, and love the sport ‚Äî not just win races.

I am a USATF Level 1 and NFHS Certified Track and Field Coach. These certifications help guide how I keep athletes safe, teach proper technique, and plan smart training.
