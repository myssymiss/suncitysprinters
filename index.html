Join the Sprinters

Our Mission

To empower athletes of all ages and abilities in the El Paso area through comprehensive track and field programs, fostering a culture of excellence, sportsmanship, and community engagement while promoting physical fitness and personal development.

Our Vision

To be a leading youth track and field organization in our region, known for integrity, athlete growth, and creating positive lifelong impact through sport.

Core Values

  • • Discipline & Commitment
  • • Sportsmanship & Respect
  • • Confidence & Accountability
  • • Community & Teamwork
