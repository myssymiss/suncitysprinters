Our Mission

Why we do what we do on the track and in our city.

To provide comprehensive track and field programs that develop athletes of all skill levels. Promote excellence and character, and build a lifelong love for the sport while serving as a positive force in the El Paso community through outreach and mentorship.

Discipline ▾
Consistency matters—showing up, finishing reps, and doing the small things right every day.
Confidence ▾
Trusting your work—learning to compete with courage, composure, and belief in your training.
Teamwork ▾
Pushing together—supporting teammates, owning your role, and building relay-level trust.
Community ▾
Uplifting El Paso—welcoming families, removing barriers, and giving back through mentorship.

What we focus on

  • Skill development for every level
  • Positive coaching & mentorship
  • Strong character on and off track
  • Removing barriers for families
Support the Mission

Our Vision

Fueling dreams, one stride at a time

We envision a future where young athletes in El Paso have access to structured, supportive, and inspiring track and field opportunities that promote physical fitness, confidence, discipline, and teamwork.

Through community support, mentorship, and intentional development, Sun City Sprinters exists to remove barriers, uplift potential, and help athletes grow into strong competitors and responsible leaders on and off the track.

Building Confidence. Creating Opportunity.
