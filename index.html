My Coaching Philosophy ‚ñæ

At Sun City Sprinters, I coach because I truly love track and field and believe in doing things the right way. I started this program to give athletes a safe and positive place to learn, build confidence, and grow strong habits.

I focus on teaching strong basics, setting clear expectations, and training in ways that fit each athlete‚Äôs age and ability. I also ran track myself, so I understand what it feels like to train, compete, and work toward goals, and I bring that experience into every practice.

Sun City Sprinters is a nonprofit team that serves our community and helps give kids access to quality coaching in a positive environment. Our goal is to help athletes grow, build character, and love the sport not just win races.

I am a USATF Level 1 and NFHS Certified Track and Field Coach. These certifications help guide how I keep athletes safe, teach proper technique, and plan smart training.

Most importantly, I care deeply about every athlete on this team. I take coaching seriously and want every athlete to leave practice feeling proud, confident, and supported, both on the track and in life.
