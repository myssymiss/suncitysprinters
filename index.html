Join the Sprinters

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive track and field programs that develop athletes of all skill levels, promoting excellence, character, and lifelong love for the sport, while serving as a positive force in the El paso community through outreach and mentorship.

Our Vision

To empower young athletes in El Paso through track and field, fostering physical fitness, discipline, teamwork, and personal growth, while instilling values of sportmanship and community engagement.

Core Values

  • • Discipline & Commitment
  • • Sportsmanship & Respect
  • • Confidence & Accountability
  • • Community & Teamwork
